BUSINESS SIDE OF THE ITHACA APPLE FESTIVAL

This is the enthralling Business oriented side of the Ithaca Festival

It’s not hard to see why Bon Apetit magazine once recognized Ithaca as one of the “foodiest” towns in America. With over 50 restaurants in the walkable district serving a variety of cuisine and offering a selection of dining styles from farm-to-table to fine-dining, Downtown Ithaca makes a perfect setting for people to come together to dine deliciously!

Whether you’re looking for available office or retail space, financial resources, assistance with your business plan; or have questions regarding permits, zoning, or codes – the Downtown Ithaca Alliance will help you get started, and help you grow. The DIA is charged with preserving and developing the central downtown core as the region’s center for specialty shops, restaurants, arts, entertainment, housing, banking and finance, business and professional offices, as well as government and community services. We aim to help those starting a business or bringing a business to this community as well as those looking to expand through years of growth within our Downtown Business Improvement District. The health of your business' entire lifespan is important to us!

Economic Development Services

  • Property listings
  • Maintaining & updating ten year strategic plans for the Downtown business improvement district
  • Working with developers & investors to find and develop project
  • Managing market data
  • Business succession planning

Business Development Services

  • Retail & office space listings
  • Business recruitment & attraction
  • Helping existing businesses expand and grow
  • Providing start-up business assistance
  • Retail needs assessment

Contact Us

The DIA offers its services at no charge and all inquiries are confidential. Call to speak directly with Gary Ferguson, Executive Director or Business Development Director, Kristina Thelen at (607) 277-8679.

Small Business Development

Downtown Ithaca is home to over 100 shops, restaurants and service-based small businesses. We know the needs of small businesses are different than those of large companies and are also very unique to each business. We provide a number of supportive services and funding opportunities to help small businesses, new entrepreneurs, and growing businesses succeed. Some of these services include grant funding opportunities, marketing technical assistance and promotional support, and networking.
